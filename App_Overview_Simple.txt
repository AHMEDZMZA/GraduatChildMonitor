# GraduatChildMonitor - Comprehensive App Overview (Simple & Detailed)

## 📱 Detailed App Flow & User Journey

### 1. The Onboarding & Registration Phase
- **Welcome & Introduction**: The app greets users with friendly screens explaining its purpose: helping parents monitor, understand, and support their child's developmental milestones.
- **Account Creation (Signup/Login)**: Parents can create an account using a standard email and password or utilize quick social logins (Google and Facebook).
- **Password Recovery**: A fully guided flow is available if a password is forgotten. It includes requesting a reset, receiving a secure OTP (One Time Password), verifying the OTP, confirming the new password, and a success confirmation screen.

### 2. Child Profile & Initial Assessment
- **Adding Child Data**: Parents start by creating a profile for their child, entering basic information like age and name.
- **Condition Selection (Crucial Step)**: 
  - **Known Condition**: If the parent already knows the child has a specific developmental condition (e.g., ADHD, Autism), they can select this path. The app will then tailor its questions and activities specifically for this condition.
  - **Unknown Condition**: If the parent is unsure why their child is experiencing delays or behavioral changes, the app offers an "Unknown Condition" path. 
- **The Testing Phase**: Depending on the previous choice, the app presents a series of interactive questions (`Test Questions Known` or `Test Questions Unknown`). These tests evaluate the child's current level. Once finished, a `Test Complete` screen provides the initial results.

### 3. The Daily Routine (Today's Plan)
This is the core daily feature of the app. Based on the test results, the app generates a personalized daily schedule.
- **Today's Overview**: A screen showing all tasks assigned for the current day.
- **Categorized Activities**: Activities are split into specific types, such as:
  - *Physical Activities*: Exercises or physical games to help motor skills.
  - *Parent-Child Activities*: Interactive tasks designed to build communication and bonding between the parent and the child.
- **Step-by-Step Guidance**: When an activity is clicked (`Activity Details`), the app breaks it down into easy-to-follow steps (`Activity Steps`). 
- **Completion Tracking**: Once finished, parents mark the task as done (`Activity Done`), which feeds directly into the child's progress report.

### 4. Continuous Tracking & Learning
- **Progress Tracking**: A dedicated section that takes all the completed activities and test scores and turns them into visual charts. Parents can clearly see improvements over weeks or months.
- **Educational Articles**: A library filled with resources, articles, and tips written by experts. This empowers parents with knowledge on how to handle specific situations.
- **Chat/Support**: If parents feel lost or need advice, an in-app chat feature is available to connect them with support or automated guidance.
- **Smart Notifications**: The app sends gentle reminders to ensure parents don't forget to complete the daily plan or take follow-up tests.

---

## ✨ Detailed Features Breakdown

- **Adaptive Assessment Engine**: The app doesn't use a "one size fits all" approach. It completely changes its behavior based on whether the child's condition is known or unknown.
- **Micro-Stepping for Activities**: Activities aren't just titles; they are broken down into actionable, bite-sized steps so parents know exactly *how* to execute them with their child.
- **Multi-Category Daily Plans**: Ensures a balanced development by mixing physical tasks with cognitive and bonding tasks.
- **Secure Authentication System**: Includes robust OTP verification for password resets, ensuring user data is highly secure.
- **Visual Progress Dashboards**: Transforms complex developmental data into easy-to-read charts.
- **Push Notifications with Timezones**: Ensures reminders are sent at the correct local time for the parent.

---

## 🚀 Why is this App Exceptional? (Advantages)

- **Deeply Personalized Experience**: By separating "Known" and "Unknown" conditions right from the start, the app provides highly accurate and relevant support that feels custom-made for every single child.
- **Action-Oriented**: Instead of just telling parents what is wrong, it provides a *Today Plan* that tells them exactly what to do to fix it or improve the situation.
- **Reduces Parent Anxiety**: By breaking down large developmental hurdles into small daily steps and providing educational articles, the app makes parenting a child with developmental needs much less overwhelming.
- **Saves Time**: Busy parents don't need to research activities online; the app provides a curated list every single morning.
- **Holistic Approach**: It focuses on the child's physical growth, cognitive growth, and emotional bond with the parent simultaneously.
